Why groundwater flooding is a problem

Groundwater flooding is a problem because:

  • water will usually come up through the ground or the floor
  • It usually happens days, weeks or months after lots of rain and can last a long time
  • it often happens in the places you don't expect, like on the side of a hill

High groundwater levels can make other types of flooding worse, as water cannot drain away.

What to do next

You cannot completely protect your property from flooding, but there are things you can do to reduce the:

  • the amount of water that gets in
  • the damage from flood water
  • the cost and time of cleaning up after a flood

You can:

  • Read about how to protect yourself from future flooding
  • Make a personal flood plan
  • Read about adapting your home or business to flooding
  • Sign up to get flood warnings
  • Read a more about groundwater flooding

You should check your flood risk regularly because the information may change.
